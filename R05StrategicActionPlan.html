Strategic Action Planning Roadmap

Mapping Strategic Goals to Executable Actions (2026-2029)

Executive Summary

This framework converts the organization's high-level strategic outcomes into granular, accountable action plans across seven key pillars: Growth, Social Impact, Technology, Financial Performance, Governance, and People Development. The primary focus is achieving significant top-line growth (retaining clients and driving £3m operating profit), enhancing corporate social responsibility (embedding ESG), and strengthening internal foundations (governance, financial controls, and talent management). The immediate 90-day plan prioritizes critical organizational structure decisions, key recruitment, financial control implementation, and strategy finalization to establish a robust foundation for the 2026/27 fiscal year.
