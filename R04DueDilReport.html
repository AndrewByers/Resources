1. Executive Summary

IUF Group is a UK-based technology "supergroup" specializing in advancing human experiences through integrated AV, IoT, and experiential digital media. Founded by Josh Bunce in 2008 as Inurface Media, the group formally consolidated under IUF Group Ltd in December 2022.

HQ: Wimbledon, London
Entities: 6 Legal Entities / 7 Brands
Headcount: ~29 - 50+ (est.)
Key Clients: Frasers Group, Sports Direct, Flannels, boohoo
Global Presence: UK, USA, South Africa

3. Leadership & Talent

Josh Bunce — Founder & CEO

A serial entrepreneur with 12+ years in the AV sector. Bunce transitioned the firm from a signage company to a diverse tech collective. He is a frequent speaker at major events like ISE 2026 and is the primary strategic driver of the group.

Key Team Members

  • Richard Henry Boydell: Director (Energy market specialist).
  • Bradd Peacock: Senior Sales (Based in Cape Town, SA).

4. Technologies & IP

Stadium IPTV Solution (Launched Oct 2025)

Powered by Navori Labs' StiX 3800, this platform enables uniform content distribution, live streaming, and revenue monetization across large-scale venues.
